How to Play

Guess The Number is a two-player game where each player takes turns trying to find the correct hidden number.

Rules

  • Join a lobby or create your own.
  • A random number will be generated between 1 and 100, and it's your job to guess it
  • Take turns making guesses
  • Use the hints given after your or the opponent's turn to make your next guess
  • The first player to guess the correct number wins!

Tips

Tips will indicate if your guess is too low, or too high. If your guess is 10 or less below/above the random number, you will see a 🔥 next to the hint
